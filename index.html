The Collage Web App

As soon as the popup appears, please click Allow. After you've spoken a command.

To use this web app, you need to first click the "Start" button. Then, say "Take my selfie". That will allow you to say it, and then once you click the popup on the top left side of your screen, it will convert that command to text-to-speech. Then, your camera will turn on and you can take a screenshot of the photo.
